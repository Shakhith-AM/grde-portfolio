## What is GRDE?

Most investors know **how much** to save ‚Äî but not **how to split it**.

The 25x retirement rule gives you a target number. But it never tells you what percentage goes to gold, what goes to equity, what goes to debt. And with Indian inflation running at 6‚Äì7%, the 4% withdrawal rate behind the 25x rule doesn't hold.

**GRDE solves this.** It's an asset allocation framework built on architectural principles ‚Äî layering your portfolio like a building:

| Layer | Asset | Role | Architectural Analogy |
| ----- | ----- | ---- | --------------------- |
| **G** | Gold | Preservation and hedge | Foundation |
| **R** | REITs / InvITs | Income and structural binding | Walls |
| **D** | Debt | Stability and superstructure | Superstructure |
| **E** | Equity | Growth and function | Aesthetics and life |

## Features

- **Age-Based Allocation** ‚Äî Enter your age, get an instant GRDE split tailored to your life stage

- **Investment Safety Ratio (ISR)** ‚Äî A single number that tells you if your portfolio is structurally sound

- **Beaver Dam Philosophy** ‚Äî Inspired by how beavers layer their dams: foundation first, then walls, then reinforcement. Each layer has a purpose.

- **No login. No ads. Completely free.**
